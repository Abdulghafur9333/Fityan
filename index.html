About FITYAN

FITYAN is a transformative youth program rooted in Islamic principles. We aim to nurture the next generation by equipping them with faith, life skills, and confidence to thrive in the modern world through workshops, creative projects, and community events.

Our Programs

Salah Workshop

Practical guidance on prayer, with emphasis on khushū‘ and consistency.

Islamic Etiquette

Understanding adab in daily life: hygiene, manners, and respectful behavior.

Qur’an Contemplation

Learn Tafsir and Tadabbur techniques to connect deeply with the Qur’an.

Digital Literacy

Build tech skills and understand responsible digital behavior and safety.

Bakery & Cakery (Girls Only)

Fun, hands-on baking with entrepreneurship training for young girls.

Public Speaking

Gain confidence and structure in delivering Islamic and personal talks.

Creative Writing

Explore Islamic storytelling, journaling, and expression through writing.

FITYAN Camps

Immersive camps with spiritual, life skills, and team-building activities.
