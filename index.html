About FITYAN

FITYAN is a transformative youth program rooted in Islamic principles. We aim to nurture the next generation by equipping them with faith, life skills, and confidence to thrive in the modern world through workshops, creative projects, and community events.

Salah Workshop

In this workshop, participants will receive practical guidance on how to perform Salah (prayer) with sincerity and focus. Emphasis will be placed on the inner aspect of prayer (khushū') and maintaining consistency in performing it regularly.

Islamic Etiquette

This program teaches the Islamic etiquette (adab) in various aspects of daily life, including personal hygiene, manners with others, and respectful behavior in different situations.

Quran Contemplation

Participants will learn techniques for understanding (Tafsir) and deep reflection (Tadabbur) of the Quran to establish a stronger spiritual connection with the divine text.

Bakery Cakery

A hands-on baking program exclusively for girls, combining culinary skills with basic entrepreneurship training in a fun and engaging environment.

Public Speaking

This program helps participants develop confidence and structure in public speaking, with a focus on delivering both Islamic and personal talks effectively.

Creative Writing

Explore various forms of writing including Islamic storytelling, personal journaling, and creative expression through structured writing exercises.

Fityan Camps

Immersive residential camps that combine spiritual development, practical life skills training, and team-building activities in a supportive environment.

Digital Literacy

Teaches essential technology skills while emphasizing responsible digital behavior, online safety, and ethical use of digital platforms.
